# About Colabora

Colabora is a collaborative drafting application built for groups that make decisions together—not just documents alone.

## Test and preview status

Colabora is currently in an **open test phase**. The software is under active development; features may change, fail, or be removed without notice. **Test users participate at their own risk.** Please do not store sensitive or confidential information in the app—**treat anything you upload as potentially public**, and read our [Terms of use](/info/terms) and [Privacy policy](/info/privacy) before registering.

## What we do

We help communities, organizations, and teams write and agree on documents with proposals, discussion, voting, and governance structures built in. Real-time collaboration and transparent decision-making are at the core of the product.

## Democratic intelligence

Colabora goes beyond a shared editor: structured governance, representative workflows, meeting protocols, and audit-friendly processes support how groups actually work.

## Open development

The project is actively developed. Technical documentation lives in the repository README and architecture docs.
