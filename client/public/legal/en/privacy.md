## Important — test and preview environment

Colabora is running as an **experimental test**. This policy describes how data is handled during that phase. It is **not** a statement that production-grade privacy or security measures are in place.

**You must assume that any information you provide may become public** — including account details, documents, comments, votes, and messages. **Do not submit data you are not willing to have exposed.**

There has been **no formal data protection impact assessment (DPIA)** or equivalent review for this test deployment. Security controls, access restrictions, encryption practices, and operational procedures **may be incomplete or absent**. Use the service **at your own risk**.

## Who we are

Colabora ("we", "us") operates a collaborative drafting platform with democratic governance features. During the test period we process data only to operate and improve the preview.

## Data we may process

During the test, we may store or process:

- **Account data** — name, email address, password (stored hashed), and profile preferences.
- **Content you create** — documents, comments, votes, organization membership, and related activity.
- **Technical data** — IP address, browser type, device information, and logs generated during operation.
- **Communications** — messages you send via contact forms or error reports.

Because this is a test environment, **we do not guarantee confidentiality** for any of these categories.

## How we use data during the test

We use data to:

- Operate, debug, and evaluate the Colabora preview.
- Authenticate users and investigate abuse or errors.
- Send transactional emails where configured (for example invitations or password reset).
- Respond to contact or support requests.

We **do not** represent that processing meets GDPR, CCPA, or other regulatory requirements during this test phase.

## Security and data protection

**No production security or data protection standard applies.** The test deployment may lack:

- Hardened infrastructure and monitoring
- Restricted access controls and least-privilege administration
- Regular penetration testing or security audits
- Documented subprocessors, transfer mechanisms, or retention schedules
- A completed data protection assessment

**Treat all data you provide as potentially accessible to operators, other testers, or unauthorized parties.**

## Retention and deletion

Data may be retained for the duration of the test and deleted at any time without notice. Backups, if any, are not guaranteed. You may request deletion by contacting us, but we cannot promise complete removal from all systems.

## Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have statutory privacy rights. During this voluntary test, we will try to respond to reasonable requests, but **we do not guarantee** the ability to access, correct, erase, or export data, especially if the service is reset or discontinued.

To make a request, contact {{contactEmail}}.

## Cookies and local storage

We use essential cookies and browser local storage for authentication and preferences (for example language). We do not use third-party advertising trackers during the test.

## International transfers

Test infrastructure and providers may be located in various jurisdictions. No adequacy or standard contractual safeguards are represented for this preview.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the test evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above. A future production launch would require a separate, reviewed privacy policy.
